# Customer Behaviour Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, spending behavior, and retention trends. This project transforms raw customer data into meaningful business insights that help organizations improve customer engagement, marketing effectiveness, and revenue strategies.

---

## Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is essential for any business aiming to improve customer satisfaction and long-term profitability. Raw transactional data alone cannot reveal patterns or trends.

This project focuses on analyzing customer data and presenting it through a visually interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders quickly identify important trends and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing patterns
* Identify repeat and high-value customers
* Understand spending habits and order trends
* Track customer growth and retention
* Support strategic marketing and customer engagement decisions

---

## Tech Stack

* **Power BI** — Data visualization & dashboard development
* **Power Query** — Data cleaning & transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** — KPI calculations & measures
* **Excel / CSV** — Data sources

---

## Dashboard Features

### Customer Overview

* Total Customers
* Active vs Inactive Customers
* New vs Returning Customers
* Customer growth trends over time

### Purchasing Behaviour

* Purchase frequency analysis
* Average customer spending
* Order distribution patterns
* Identification of high-value customers

### Revenue Insights

* Revenue contribution by customer segments
* Top revenue-generating customers
* Customer spending distribution

### Regional & Demographic Insights

* Location-wise customer distribution
* Region-wise revenue contribution
* Customer concentration analysis

### Time-Based Trends

* Monthly and yearly purchase trends
* Seasonal buying patterns
* Customer retention analysis
